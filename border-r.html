border-radius

The border-radius CSS property rounds the corners of an element's outer border edge.

border-radius: 30px;
border-radius: 25% 10%;
border-radius: 10% 30% 50% 70%;
border-radius: 10px 100px / 120px;
border-radius: 50% 20% / 10% 40%;
